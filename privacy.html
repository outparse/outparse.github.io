Legal & Privacy

Welcome to Outparse. We care deeply about your privacy, your rights, and your experience. Please take a moment to read through this page so you know how your data is handled and what to expect as you use our platform.

Disclaimer

Outparse is an evolving project. Features may change, improve, or be removed over time. While we aim to provide helpful and accurate suggestions, we can't guarantee every correction or recommendation will be perfect or appropriate in every context.

Use your own judgment when reviewing suggestions. Outparse is a support tool—not a replacement for your voice, style, or professional advice.

Privacy and Data Use

We believe in privacy by design. Here's how we handle your information:

Guest Users (No Account)

  • We do not collect or store any data from users who choose not to create an account.
  • All app usage and documents stay only on your device.
  • If you clear your browser or app cache, all your documents, settings, and preferences will be permanently lost.
  • We do not and cannot access this data.

Registered Users (With Account)

If you choose to create an account:

  • Your documents and writing history are stored securely in the cloud, so you can access them from anywhere.
  • Your preferences (e.g., tone settings, language variant) are also saved for a smoother experience.
  • We do not sell or share your data with any third parties. Ever.

Deleting Your Data

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings page. This will remove your documents and preferences from our servers.

If you need confirmation or have any concerns, send an email to support@outparse.com

Respecting Your Trust

We are committed to building a writing tool that respects your privacy, your creativity, and your time. We will always communicate openly about updates to our policies and practices.

If you have questions, concerns, or ideas about how we can do better, don't hesitate to reach out.
